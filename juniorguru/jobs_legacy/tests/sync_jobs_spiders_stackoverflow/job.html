Job description

We are looking for a curious Solution Engineer (M/F/X), who will help us build digital retail/in-store solutions for brands all over Europe.

Your tasks:

  • As a determined and communicative team member with a passion for new technologies, you will be significantly contributing to the further development of existing customer projects

  • You will develop new platforms using state-of-the-art technology stacks or are responsible for their relaunch

  • You will contribute to the expansion of customer-specific CI and CD processes, the recording, and evaluation of new requirements in cooperation with the customers as well as the installation and testing of version and security updates

  • Thanks to your highly analytical and problem-solving thinking, you also ensure the smooth operation of the staging and production environments

This profile will win us over:

  • You are familiar with an object-oriented programming style and knowledgable about at least one programming language

  • You have a basic understanding of S.O.L.I.D principles

  • You are good with either frontend or backend but know the basics of both

  • You will be able to manage your daily business thanks to your experienced handling of RESTful backend services

  • You are keen to learn more than one programming language and also open to new technologies

That's what QUAJOO offers you:

  • Participation in a continuously growing company with flat hierarchies and agile development processes

  • Exciting projects with modern technologies and a lot of potential for your personal development

  • A job in the city center of Leipzig

  • Your work will, of course, be reasonably salaried and additionally rewarded in case of project success and goal achievement

  • Regular team events as well as free snacks, coffee, etc. will ensure your future-all-round carefree package

Life at QUAJOO GmbH

About QUAJOO GmbH

Most of our developers started their career in classic software development from scratch or as add-on-projects is one of our specialties. We do apply the latest development environments and also have a huge repertoire of legacy knowledge that comes in handy in systems integration. Our team is ready to manage complex integration and development projects, starting from scratch or in re-iterations to existing systems.

Benefits

  • Fair salary and performance bonuses
  • Flexible working hours
  • Show your strength at paddling tours and sports events
  • Central location with great public transport connections
  • Free drinks, fruits and ice cream
  • Modern technical equipment
  • Get inspiration at user groups and talks
  • Be together with the colleagues at afterworks
  • Free leasing bike
